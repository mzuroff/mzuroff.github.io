ABOUT ME

Farfel

Aussiedoodles, also known as Aussiepoos or Australian Shepherd Poodle mixes, are a hybrid breed resulting from crossing an Australian Shepherd and a Poodle. They are known for their intelligence, high energy, and affectionate nature. Their coats can vary in type (straight, wavy, or curly) and come in a wide range of colors and patterns, often including the distinctive merle pattern inherited from the Australian Shepherd.
