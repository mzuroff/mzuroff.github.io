ABOUT ME

Stechie

Golden retrievers are a popular breed known for their friendly, intelligent, and playful nature. They are typically medium-sized dogs with a beautiful golden coat and a gentle temperament, making them excellent family pets. Breed information from The Spruce Pets says they are eager to please and relatively easy to train, enjoying activities like swimming and retrieving.
